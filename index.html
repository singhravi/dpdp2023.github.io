Exercise 1: Identify the Data Fiduciary

Scenario: A citizen uses the government's online portal to apply for a driving license. The portal is developed by a private IT company contracted by the Transport Department. The application data is stored on cloud servers managed by another vendor.

Question: Who is the Data Fiduciary?

‚úÖ Correct Answer: C - The Transport Department

Explanation: The Data Fiduciary is the entity that determines the purpose and means of processing. The Transport Department decides WHY to collect data (driving license issuance) and HOW to collect it (online portal). The IT company and cloud provider are Data Processors - they process data on behalf of the Data Fiduciary. The department remains responsible even when vendors handle the actual processing.

Key Learning: Don't confuse technical operations with legal responsibility. The entity that owns the service/function is the Data Fiduciary.
